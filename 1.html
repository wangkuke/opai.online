Course Overview

This course will guide you step by step to master YouTube Shorts creation and monetization skills, using AI tools to efficiently produce content and quickly meet YPP requirements for earning revenue.

  • Three learning phases: Account Setup, Content Creation, Monetization Guidance
  • AI Creation Skills: Master AI tools to boost your content productivity
  • Monetization Guidance: Detailed explanation of YPP requirements and process

Phase 1: Account Setup

Understand the YouTube platform and monetization paths, complete account registration and basic settings.

  • Learn YouTube's basic rules and main monetization methods
  • Understand YPP requirements and thresholds
  • Successfully register and set up your YouTube account

Phase 2: Content Creation

Identify your content direction, learn AI creation skills, and complete your first Shorts video production and publishing.

  • Explore trending AI Shorts niches and find your content focus
  • Master AI tools for video creation and learn to write effective prompts
  • Produce and publish your first AI-generated Shorts video

Phase 3: Monetization

Establish a content production workflow, continuously optimize video quality, meet YPP requirements, and start earning revenue.

  • Build an efficient AI content creation workflow for consistent video updates
  • Optimize video content to increase watch time and engagement
  • Meet YPP requirements, complete the application process, and start earning
