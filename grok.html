About Grok

Grok is an AI model developed by XAI (Elon Musk's AI company), designed for understanding complex problems, generating high-quality content, and intelligent conversation. Grok not only handles multi-turn conversations but also performs knowledge reasoning, fact retrieval, code generation, and more. It is widely used in content creation, intelligent assistants, education, research, and other fields.

Core capabilities include: natural language understanding, multi-turn conversation, knowledge reasoning, content generation, code assistance, and more.
