About Invidious

Invidious (yewtu.be) is an alternative YouTube frontend that is open source, ad-free, and privacy-friendly. It allows you to watch YouTube videos, manage subscriptions, and explore content without being tracked or shown ads. Perfect for privacy-conscious users, researchers, and anyone seeking a cleaner YouTube experience.

Key features: Ad-free viewing, privacy protection, anonymous subscriptions, RSS support, no registration, open source, and more.

Key Features

  • Ad-free and tracking-free YouTube experience
  • Privacy-respecting: no Google account or login required
  • Anonymous subscriptions and RSS feeds
  • Open source and community-driven
  • Mobile-friendly and responsive design
  • Completely free, no registration, no ads

Technical Advantages

  • Decentralized, multiple public instances available
  • Strong privacy, no user tracking or data collection
  • Customizable, open API, and extensible features

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How does Invidious work?

Invidious acts as a proxy between you and YouTube, fetching and displaying videos without ads or tracking. You can watch, search, and subscribe without a Google account.

Is Invidious free to use?

Yes, Invidious is completely free and open source. No registration or payment required.

Is my data safe and private?

Absolutely. Invidious does not track users, collect personal data, or require login.

Can I use Invidious on mobile?

Yes, yewtu.be is mobile-friendly. You can also scan the QR code above to open it on your phone.
